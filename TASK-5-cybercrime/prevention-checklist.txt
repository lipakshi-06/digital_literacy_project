Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students 

 Do’s (Safety Tips) 

• Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and update them regularly  

• Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and 
banking apps  

• Verify links and sender details before clicking on emails or messages  

• Keep your devices and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities  

• Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid accessing sensitive accounts on public 
Wi-Fi  

• Regularly review privacy settings on social media platforms  

 Financial / UPI Safety Tips 

• Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  

• Do not approve unknown “collect requests” or scan random QR codes  

• Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment  

• Avoid using unofficial apps or links for financial transactions 

 Don’ts 

• Don’t click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments  

• Don’t share personal information like address, phone number, or ID 
proofs publicly  

• Don’t respond to messages offering prizes, jobs, or urgent money requests  

• Don’t reuse the same password across multiple platforms  

Report Cybercrime 

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately: 

• National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in  

• Helpline Number: 1930
